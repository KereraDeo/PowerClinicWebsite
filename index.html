First Prenatal appointment

The first appointment is often scheduled for around 8-12 weeks of pregnancy. During this visit, the healthcare provider will: - Confirm the pregnancy. - Review the individual's medical history. - Perform a physical examination.

Ongoing Prenatal appointment

Prenatal care typically involves regular check-ups, which become more frequent as the pregnancy progresses (e.g., monthly, bi-weekly, and weekly visits).these appointments, the healthcare provider will: Monitor the baby's growth and development.

Nutrition and Lifestyle Guidance

Throughout pregnancy, healthcare providers offer guidance on nutrition, exercise, and lifestyle choices. They may recommend specific dietary supplements like folic acid and iron.
